ENGL 1100: Introduction to University Writing

ENGL 1100: Introduction to University Writing

This course introduces students to the practices of reading and writing in scholarly contexts. Students will read and analyze scholarly journal articles from a variety of disciplines. They will also develop their abilities to compose in the genres.

Pre-Requirements

None.
